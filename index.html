Godrej Nupur Natural Henna Based Hair color

Application

Step 01

Apply petroleum jelly or oil to keep skin free of stains.

Step 02

*Nature Black- Mix entire powder with 60 ml lukewarm water

*Nature Brown- Mix entire powder with 90 ml lukewarm water

*Burgundy- Mix entire powder with 60 ml lukewarm water

Step 03

Keep it for 30 Mins

Step 04

*Natural Black & Burgundy- Wash hair after 30 minutes

*Nature Brown- Wash hair after 60 mintues
